We may use the information that we collect for any of the following purposes:

  • Provide, maintain and improve our services

You have control over the information that you share with Hovr subject to certain limitations.

Hovr’s Services have been built in a way to minimize the amount of information that is stored within them. Nonetheless, if you want to review, update, change or delete the information that you have shared with Hovr, including deletion of your account, you may send a request to do so to support@hovrcarpool.com.
